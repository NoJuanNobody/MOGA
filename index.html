About Moga

Moga aims to capture those essential elements that made yoga appealing to men in ancient India. Yoga has continued to develop over the millennia, and Moga encompasses this through a blend of new approaches, techniques and methods. Everyone has an individual spark, a personality, that can flourish through yoga over time. Yoga is now common in mainstream sports. The meaning of fitness is expanded in yoga to integrate mind and body through the action of breath and conscious movement. An improved fitness gained in yoga can then be applied productively in competitive sports.

Yoga is restorative to the mind and to the body. The therapeutic effects of yoga can be felt as renewed energy. Yoga also develops our energy through focused practice.

Our success depends on our performance in all spheres of life. Performance benefits of yoga are well documented. Increased energy and fitness only partly describe the performance benefits from yoga in all aspects of life, social, home, work and sports.
